						<p><font size="-2">Episodes 177&amp;178 - The Clip Show (a.k.a. The Chronicle)<br>
							
						pc: 921 season 9, episodes 21&amp;22<br>
							
						Broadcast date: May 14, 1998<br>
							<br>
							
						Written by Darin Henry<br>
							
						Directed by Andy Ackerman<br>
							<br>
							
						-------------------------------------------------------------------------------<br>
							<br>
							
						The Cast<br>
							
						Regulars:<br>
							
						Jerry Seinfeld ......................&nbsp; Jerry Seinfeld<br>
							
						Jason Alexander .................. George Costanza<br>
							
						Julia Louis-Dreyfus .............. Elaine Benes<br>
							
						Michael Richards .................. Cosmo Kramer<br>
							<br>
							
						==================================================================<br>
							<br>
							
						*Part 1 (only cut up for syndication)<br>
							<br>
							
						[Jerry's Apartment]<br>
							<br>
							
						GEORGE: (checking his watch) You know if we are gonna see this movie we gotta get moving.<br>
							<br>
							
						(Kramer is seen exiting his Apartment through Jerry's open door)<br>
							<br>
							
						ELAINE: Let's Roll (hand motions to hurry up)<br>
							<br>
							
						(Kramer enters through the threshold)<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER: Come on people, previews, Shnell! Shnell!<br>
							<br>
							
						(Kramer, George and Elaine leave Jerry's and start down the hall; Jerry walking door the door putting on his coat, grabs the nob to leave then turns around and notices &quot;us&quot; the viewers)<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: Oh, Hello. Nine years, seems like a long time doesn't it? It is, and we've packed a lot in the four of us, it seems like every week a whole new set of problems would just crop up outta nowhere...except for summer where nothing seemed to happen for months at a time. Anyway the point is over the last nine years...<br>
							<br>
							
						GEORGE: (from the hallway off camera) Jerry were gonna miss the movie.<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (toward the door yelling) I'm comin'!<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER (from the hallway off camera) Previews Jerry!<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (toward the door yelling) Just go already! (back to the viewers) Over the last nine years we've had a lot experiences. And I'll tell ya, when you stop and add em all up. That's what you realize. Watch this.<br>
							<br>
							
						ELAINE: (from the hallway off camera) Jerry?!<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (toward the door yelling) SHUTUP!! (turn back toward the viewers and smilies and we break into clips)<br>
							<br>
							<br>
							
						*List of all the clips shown and their corresponding episode to be added later<br>
							<br>
							<br>
							<br>
							
						*Part 2 (Only shown during syndication)<br>
							<br>
							
						[Jerry's Apartment]<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: Wow Nine years, A hundred and eighty shows (turns and looks toward his kitchen) same twelve boxes of cereal, and millions and millions of dollars.<br>
							<br>
							
						(kramer runs into the locked door)<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER: Jerry you in there?<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (toward the door) Busy (toward the viewers) We've made a lot of friends, shared a lot of laughs, often at the expense of others, and slept with.. well who's counting?<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER: (trying to get in again) Ah man why's the door locked.<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: I'm coming. Anyway watch this.<br>
							<br>
							
						(Jerry opens the door and Kramer falls)<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER: (getting back up) Jeez, (looking around) who you talking to?<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (walking out the door) No one.<br>
							<br>
							
						KRAMER: (starts his way out closing the door; notices the viewers) Oh, Hello.<br>
							<br>
							
						JERRY: (from the hallway off camera) KRAMER!<br>
							<br>
							
						(Kramer smilies as he exits; Clips begin)<br>
							<br>
							<br>
							<br>
